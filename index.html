Our Services

Agro foods built on integrity

Farm-Fresh Sourcing

We partner with trusted growers to select only the finest raw ingredients — spices, grains, and produce at peak quality.

Agro Processing

Professional processing of grains, blends, and agro products — preserving nutrients and quality from farm to finished pack.

Consultancy Services

Expert guidance on agro-food production, business setup, and scaling — practical advice tailored to your operation.

Value Addition

Turning raw farm produce into higher-value food products — better shelf life, stronger margins, and ready markets for growers.

Food Safety

Our Products

Mayopp's Agro Foods — premium natural ingredients milled, blended, and packed for wholesome everyday meals.

Weanimix

A nutritious blend of soya beans, groundnuts, maize, rice, sorghum, and wheat — milled and packed for wholesome everyday meals.

Fukonte

A combination of two beloved local foods — fufu and kokonte — made from carefully selected cassava, plantain, and cocoyam for authentic Ghanaian meals.

Natural Spices

A carefully balanced blend of rosemary, coriander, fennel, bay leaf, black pepper, onion, ginger, cloves, and more — for rich, natural flavour in every dish.

Fish Powder

Finely milled dried fish powder that adds deep, savoury umami to soups, stews, and traditional Ghanaian dishes. 100% natural and shelf-ready.

Shrimp Powder

Premium dried shrimp, ground to a fine powder — a classic seasoning for authentic coastal and inland Ghanaian cooking. 100% natural.

Groundnut

Smooth, rich groundnut paste made from quality peanuts — perfect for spreads, cooking, and everyday nourishment. Available in 1.2 kg packs.

Meet the maker

Built by hand.
Powered by heart.

Mayopp KMJ Ventures began in a family kitchen — where patience, tradition, and the finest natural ingredients came together to create something extraordinary.

Led by founder and CEO Mabel Konadu Boahen, Mayopp's Agro Foods carries that same spirit into every jar, pack, and batch we produce. We believe the best food starts with respect for the land and ends with joy at your table.

  • 100% natural ingredient commitment — no artificial fillers
  • Small-batch processing for consistent quality and taste
  • Family-owned operations with personal accountability
  • Growing catalog of spices, blends, and specialty agro products
